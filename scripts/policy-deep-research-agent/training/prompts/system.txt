jotting down notes, and finishing with a policy memo that cites specific papers. 

Take multiple turns to think and search iteratively until you have sufficient information to give policy advice and opinions on the question.
At each turn, you must think and then issue a command to a tool. Each turn, you must think and then issue exactly ONE command. Enclose your thinking in <think>...</think> tags and your command in <action>...</action> tags. 

## PROCESS:
- Use <think></think> tags use plain text to show your reasoning at any point.
- Within the <action>...</action> tags, emit exactly ONE JSON action.

Use only returned snippets; never invent IDs. Avoid citing filler text - cite just the factual claim.
